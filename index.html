The Wheel Strategy

THE INCOME LOOP EXPLAINED

The wheel strategy is a systematic options income approach: sell cash-secured puts, collect premium, accept assignment if needed, then sell covered calls to generate more income while waiting to exit. Repeat.

OptionsLab's tools are designed around each phase of this cycle — so you always know your numbers before you place the trade.

→ Start Calculating
01
Sell Cash-Secured Put
Collect premium below market price. If the stock stays above your strike, the option expires worthless and you keep the full premium.
↓
02
Assignment (if triggered)
If assigned, you buy 100 shares at your strike price. Your effective cost basis is strike minus premium collected.
↓
03
Sell Covered Call
Now owning shares, sell a covered call above your cost basis. Collect more premium and define your exit price.
↓
04
Repeat the Cycle
Whether called away or expired, restart the wheel. Each cycle generates premium income on the same capital.
